Privacy Policy

Clear information about how the game handles data.

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lucky Surf Bingo ("Lucky Surf Bingo," "we," "us," or "the app") handles information when you use the game.

Information You Provide

Lucky Surf Bingo does not require you to create an account, sign in, provide a phone number, or submit an email address to play. If you contact us by email, we receive the information you choose to include in your message so that we can respond to your request.

Game Progress and Preferences

The app may store game progress, level status, scores, coins, star ratings, sound settings, and similar preferences locally on your device. This information supports your game experience and is not intended to identify you personally.

Advertising and Device Information

The app may display advertising. Advertising service providers may process limited device and technical information, such as device type, operating system, app version, advertising identifiers, approximate region, ad impressions, and interaction data, to deliver, limit, secure, and measure ads. Where required, iOS will request your permission before an app accesses data used to track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

Analytics, Diagnostics, and Security

We or our service providers may process basic diagnostics and technical events, including crash reports, performance information, app version, device model, operating system version, and ad loading status. This information is used to maintain compatibility, prevent abuse, improve stability, and understand whether app features work as intended.

How Information Is Shared

We do not sell your personal information. Limited information may be processed by service providers that support advertising, diagnostics, security, or app infrastructure. These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy practices and only for the services they provide.

Data Retention

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you reset the game data, if that option is available, or delete the app. Service providers may retain technical or advertising data for the periods described in their own policies or as required by law.

Your Choices

  • You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings.
  • You can limit personalized advertising through your device settings where available.
  • You can remove locally stored game data by deleting the app from your device.
  • You may contact us with privacy questions using the email address below.

Children’s Privacy

Lucky Surf Bingo is a general-audience casual game and is not directed to children under the age required for independent consent in their region. We do not knowingly request personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

International Use

The app and its service providers may process technical information in countries other than the one where you live. Where applicable, providers use safeguards required by relevant privacy laws.

Security

We use reasonable measures intended to protect information associated with the app. However, no electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the app, service providers, or legal requirements. The revised policy will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
