Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bingo Party: Offline Games ("Bingo Party", "we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use the game.

Information We Do Not Require

Bingo Party does not require account registration, login, phone number, email sign-in, or social profile access to play the game.

Game Progress

The app may store gameplay progress, level status, coins, star ratings, settings, and similar game data locally on your device. This information is used to continue your game experience and is not used to personally identify you.

Advertising and Device Identifiers

The app may display ads. Advertising partners may process limited device information, advertising identifiers, approximate device information, ad performance data, and related technical data to load, measure, limit, and improve ads. Where required by the platform, the app asks for permission before accessing the advertising identifier.

Analytics and Diagnostics

The app or its integrated service providers may receive basic technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, crash information, and ad loading status. This information helps improve stability, compatibility, and the game experience.

Children's Privacy

Bingo Party is designed as a casual game experience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by advertising, security, analytics, or infrastructure service providers only as needed to operate, protect, measure, and improve the app.

Your Choices

You can manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You can also reset or delete local game data by removing the app from your device, or by using any reset option provided inside the app.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect app data. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy
